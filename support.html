Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot password?" on the login page. Enter your email and we'll send you a password reset link.
How do I check my savings balance?
Log in to your dashboard. Your savings balance is displayed in the "Performance Overview" section.
How do I apply for a loan?
Go to the "Loans" page and click "Apply Loan". Fill in the required information and submit your application.
What are the loan interest rates?
Interest rates vary based on the loan type and amount. Check the loan application form for current rates or contact support.
