Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

GERESU DHUKI SACCO (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our members and users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our banking system.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, National ID, and other identifying information you provide during registration.
  • Financial Information: Savings deposits, loan applications, repayments, withdrawals, and dividend distributions.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and usage data to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your SACCO membership and transactions.
  • To process savings, loans, deposits, and withdrawals.
  • To distribute dividends and communicate important updates.
  • To improve our system and user experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption, firewalls, and secure server infrastructure, to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate our system (e.g., Firebase, cloud hosting).
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our members and organization.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also request a copy of the data we hold about you by contacting us.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to authenticate users and enhance your experience. You may disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect functionality.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
